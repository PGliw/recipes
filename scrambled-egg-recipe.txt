1. Slice the becon.
2. Put the becon slices into a pan.
3. Take 3 eggs and wash them.
4. Having fried the becon, take it off the pan.
5. Remove some of the fat comming from the fied becon from the pan.
6. Crack the eggs and put them into a pan.
7. Fry and mix them for 3 minutes.
8. Voila! Bon Appetit!
